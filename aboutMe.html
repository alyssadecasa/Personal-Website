About Me

So I am aiming for a career that intersects between Technology and Mentoring Others. Currently, I define that goal as becoming a Software Engineer and moving up into a Product Manager role, but I keep my eyes open and horizons clear! No matter what career path I end up falling into, I will add value, mentor those on my team, and leave each day feeling I learned something new.
